Why Learn By Topic?

In IELTS, vocabulary makes up 25% of your score for both Speaking and Writing. Studying by topic ensures you have the right words ready for any prompt.

Thematic Grouping

Connect words logically. Remembering vocabulary is much easier when terms are linked to a central theme like 'Crime' or 'Education'.

Ready for Task 2

Each module includes sample essays and arguments (Pros & Cons) to help you generate ideas for Writing Task 2 instantly.

Interactive Flashcards

Memorize effectively. Every topic comes with built-in 3D flip cards to test your recall of English meanings and Vietnamese translations.
