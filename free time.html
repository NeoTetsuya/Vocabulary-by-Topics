Why you should schedule free time

  1. Free time becomes important to you: When you schedule your free time, you're making that activity a priority.
  2. Show others they are a priority: If you pack your calendar with work, you're telling close ones work is more important.
  3. Have something to look forward to: No matter how crazy the day gets, you always have something great afterwards.
  4. Put to better use: Setting aside time ensures that what you do with that time actually matters.
  5. Feel more in control: Knowing when you have free time helps you decide how best to spend that limited time.
